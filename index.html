About Us

samRad Health Technologies is a visionary cancer research and healthtech enterprise. Our mission is to pioneer intelligent, automated systems that empower clinicians to detect and quantify tumors from radiological imaging with precision and ease. Our AI pipelines are open, transparent, and designed to speed up diagnostics and enhance medical research.

Founded by Samuel Takwirira, a Zimbabwean PhD student in Physics at the University of New Hampshire in the United States, the initiative is rooted in a passion for radiation oncology and a heartfelt commitment to expanding access to care. Takwirira aspires to forge partnerships with hospitals across Zimbabwe and Southern Africa, uplifting cancer care through technological excellence and shared knowledge.

Our Services

AI Tumor Detection
